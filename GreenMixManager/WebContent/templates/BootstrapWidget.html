GreenMix â€‹ a IoT solution which is mainly used to implements a real time remote sensing is infrastructure in the context of plant health monitoring and maintenance system. No matter the user would be a farmer and wants to take care of his plants or a family leaving for a vacation and want monitor their beautiful flowers inside home. By means of this system they are capable of continuous monitoring over the period being away. GreenMix targets the temperature, moisture and light data to provide information to the user to take safety and timely decisions to avoid spoilage of plants. It analyse the receiving data of the former states and the current condition of the plant to make an accurate observation and also a highly-precise prediction reachable for later states. Such data can also allow the user to anticipate the health parameters like reduction in chlorophyll level especially during winter due to insufficient sunlight, prediction of dryness factor of plants.To that end, there has been devised a couple of different technical aspects from remote sensors and their embedded gateway, Cloud architecture for the collecting and data analysis purpose, to a user friendly web-based UI which provides the user with some Visualization and management facilities.
